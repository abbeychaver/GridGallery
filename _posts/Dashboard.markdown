### Dashboard Design Guidelines and Mockup

Eager to get my feet wet in UI, I turned a critical eye to the Branch Metrics dashboard. While the overall look was quite strong, I picked out inconsistent details that could make navigation difficult for users. I built a mock-up in Illustrator of the changes I wanted, and created a reference document for maintaining consistent relationships between form and function.
